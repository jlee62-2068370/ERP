OUR PURPOSE

The Everyday Responder Project (ERP) aims to promote access
to bystander medical training and boost bystander confidence
in providing simple, yet critical aspects of emergency medical
care while awaiting emergency medical services.

We teach educational materials specifically tailored to the non-
medical demographic, with the broad goal of developing sustainable
education programs for individuals throughout the Los Angeles area.
ERP emphasizes a holistic approach to medical education, uniting a
wide variety of healthcare, educational, and creative professionals
to work collaboratively in preparing everyday people for medical
emergencies.
