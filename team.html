Executive Team

Willis Tang

Co-Founder & CEO


Willis is currently a third-year undergraduate student at the University of Southern California. He is a licensed EMT-B and has extensive clinical experience from volunteering as an EMT and from working as a Wound Care Technician. With ERP, Willis hopes to promote bystander confidence in providing medical care and bolster levels of medical competency within the communities across LA.

Inaya Riaz

Co-Founder & COO


Inaya is a candidate for a M.S. in Global Medicine at the University of Southern California. Inaya has worked as an EMT since 2018, holding various positions in ambulance services, collegiate stand-by services, and disaster relief. She has a firm belief in the principle of healthcare as a public good and hopes to spend her career offering humanitarian assistance to medically vulnerable populations.
